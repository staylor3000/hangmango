How to Play hangmango — Free Hangman Alternative

hangmango is a free online word guessing game — a modern, child-friendly alternative to the classic hangman game. Guess hidden words one letter at a time across fourteen categories: Animals, Art, Cities, Countries, Food, History, Literature, Movies, Music, Mythology, Nature, Science, Sports, and Technology.

Game rules

A random word is chosen from your selected category and difficulty. The word appears as a row of blank tiles. Click a letter on the keyboard (or press a key) to guess. Correct guesses reveal the letter in the word. Wrong guesses advance the mango animation — the branch shakes, the mango loosens, falls, bounces, and finally goes splat! You get 8 wrong guesses before the mango drops.

Custom word mode

Type your own word in the "give a friend a word to guess" box. Pass the device to another player and let them guess your word — great for parties or classrooms.

Tips

Start with common vowels like E, A, and O. Then try frequent consonants like S, T, R, and N. Use the category hint to guide your guesses — an animal word with lots of letters is probably something exotic!

Educational Benefits

Word guessing games like Hangmango are proven vocabulary-building tools used by teachers worldwide. Studies in language acquisition consistently show that retrieval practice — actively recalling words letter by letter — produces stronger long-term retention than passive reading or flashcard study. Every game of Hangmango is a retrieval practice session in disguise.

For language learners, guessing words in a target language activates spelling awareness, letter frequency knowledge, and category-based vocabulary recall simultaneously — skills that transfer directly to reading and writing. The category system (Animals, Food, Science, Mythology and more) mirrors the thematic vocabulary organisation used in CEFR-aligned curricula from A1 to B2 level.

For younger learners, the category hint and difficulty levels allow teachers to scaffold exactly the right challenge level — easy words for Year 3 spelling practice, hard words for GCSE revision. The custom word mode means a teacher can input this week's spelling list and turn vocabulary homework into a game in seconds.

For classrooms, Hangmango requires no accounts, no downloads, no data collection, and no logins — it works on any device with a browser. One link shared on a classroom screen or VLE is all it takes.
