Play Nature Now 🥭

Hangmango's Nature category covers the natural world in all its breadth — landforms, weather phenomena, ecosystems, plants and natural processes. A rewarding category for anyone passionate about the environment or studying geography and science.

How to play the Nature category

Nature vocabulary includes both short everyday words (tree, lake, rain) and long scientific terms (desertification, bioluminescence). Easy words will come naturally to most players. Medium introduces specific geographical features and ecosystem types. Hard features technical environmental and geographical terminology that will challenge geography students and nature enthusiasts.

Sample nature words

  • Easy: tree, river, flower, stone, cloud, rain, wind, leaf, root, fruit, lake, beach, forest, mountain, desert
  • Medium: waterfall, glacier, volcano, canyon, estuary, mangrove, savanna, tundra, plateau, lagoon, wetland, fjord, delta, peninsula
  • Hard: biodiversity, ecosystem, deforestation, sustainability, phototropism, pollination, hibernation, mimicry, symbiosis, decomposition, permafrost, bioluminescence, desertification, afforestation

Educational value

Nature and environment vocabulary is core to GCSE Geography, Biology and Environmental Science. This category directly supports exam preparation while engaging students in topics relevant to climate change and environmental education.
