Hangmango's History category covers the vocabulary of human history — from ancient civilisations to modern political systems. Test your historical knowledge across three difficulty levels in this educational word guessing challenge.

How to play the History category

History vocabulary includes short powerful words (king, war, crown) and long conceptual terms (industrialisation, totalitarianism, decolonisation). Easy words are fundamental historical concepts everyone will know. Medium introduces specific historical movements and institutions. Hard features advanced historiographical and political science terms that will challenge history students and enthusiasts.

Sample history words

  • Easy: king, queen, war, peace, empire, castle, knight, sword, crown, throne, treaty, republic, colony, revolution, democracy
  • Medium: renaissance, reformation, enlightenment, crusades, colonialism, industrialisation, suffragette, parliament, civilisation, feudalism, monarchy, constitution, abolition
  • Hard: mesopotamia, byzantine, ottomans, conquistadors, imperialism, mercantilism, nationalism, totalitarianism, decolonisation, suffragism, abolitionism, republicanism, constitutionalism

Educational value

History vocabulary is directly assessed in GCSE and A-Level History. This category covers terminology from across the major historical periods and themes studied in UK and international secondary education.
