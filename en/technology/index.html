Play Technology Now 🥭

Hangmango's Technology category covers the vocabulary of the digital age — from everyday devices to advanced computing concepts. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a student studying computer science, this category offers a genuinely modern word game challenge.

How to play the Technology category

Technology vocabulary includes both short familiar words (phone, wifi, email) and long compound terms (cryptocurrency, blockchain, machinelearning). Easy words are everyday devices and concepts everyone will know. Medium introduces technical terminology used by developers and IT professionals. Hard features advanced computing and emerging technology terms.

Sample technology words

  • Easy: phone, laptop, screen, mouse, camera, tablet, robot, wifi, email, browser, server, pixel, cable, keyboard, battery
  • Medium: algorithm, database, software, hardware, network, firewall, encryption, interface, bandwidth, processor, bluetooth, podcast, streaming, cybersecurity, platform
  • Hard: cryptocurrency, blockchain, machinelearning, virtualreality, augmentedreality, nanotechnology, biotechnology, quantumcomputing, neuralnetwork, opensource, javascript, cloudcomputing, deeplearning, microprocessor

Educational value

Technology vocabulary is central to GCSE and A-Level Computer Science curricula. The category spans foundational concepts to emerging technologies, supporting students preparing for a world where digital literacy is essential.
