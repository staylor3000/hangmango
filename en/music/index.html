Hangmango's Music category covers genres, instruments, composers and musical concepts. From familiar jazz and rock to advanced music theory terminology, this category is a treat for music lovers and students alike.

How to play the Music category

Music words often contain unusual letter combinations — "rhythm," "jazz," "xylophone." Try vowels first, then common letters, but be prepared for surprises. Easy words are familiar genres and instruments everyone will recognise. Medium introduces orchestral instruments, music forms and compositional terms. Hard features advanced music theory vocabulary that will challenge even trained musicians.

Sample music words

  • Easy: jazz, rock, pop, blues, soul, disco, funk, piano, guitar, flute, drums, violin, harp, rhythm, melody
  • Medium: reggae, classical, trumpet, accordion, keyboard, orchestra, symphony, harmony, concert, conductor, soprano, tenor, chorus, tempo
  • Hard: polyphony, counterpoint, composition, philharmonic, improvisation, dissonance, modulation, syncopation, arpeggio, musicology, harpsichord, fortissimo, sinfonietta

Educational value

Music vocabulary supports GCSE and A-Level Music curricula. The category introduces the full range of musical terminology from beginner-friendly genres to advanced theory concepts.
