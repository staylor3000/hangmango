Movie Title Word Guessing Game

Play Movies Now 🥭

Hangmango's Movies category challenges you to guess famous film titles letter by letter. Multi-word titles have spaces revealed automatically — you only need to guess the letters. From blockbuster one-worders to classic Hollywood titles, this category is a film lover's dream.

How to play the Movies category

Movie titles often contain unusual or unexpected words — "inception," "parasite," "moonlight." Start with common vowels, then try frequent letters like T, R, S and N. Note that spaces in multi-word titles are revealed automatically, so focus on the letters. Easy difficulty features titles you'll almost certainly know. Medium introduces award-winning films that cinephiles will recognise. Hard features critically acclaimed films whose titles are less obvious.

Sample movie titles

  • Easy: jaws, grease, alien, rocky, frozen, shrek, titanic, inception, avatar, psycho, bambi, dumbo, gravity
  • Medium: gladiator, interstellar, parasite, braveheart, whiplash, moonlight, casablanca, goodfellas, memento, amelie, chinatown, spotlight
  • Hard: apocalypse now, mulholland drive, no country for old men, the shawshank redemption, pan's labyrinth, city of god, blade runner, spirited away

Educational value

Film literacy is a valuable component of cultural education. The Movies category exposes players to a curated selection of culturally significant films from across decades and genres, encouraging curiosity about cinema history.
