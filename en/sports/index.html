Play Sports Now 🥭

Hangmango's Sports category covers the full spectrum of athletic activities — from popular team sports to niche individual disciplines. Test your sporting vocabulary across three difficulty levels.

How to play the Sports category

Sports words are particularly satisfying because many have distinct letter patterns — "-ing" endings (swimming, cycling), "-ball" compounds (football, volleyball) and loan words from other languages (judo, yoga, polo). Start with common vowels and try "S" early — it appears in most sports words. Easy covers everyday sports you'll know immediately. Medium introduces less common disciplines. Hard features technical multi-syllable words that will test even the most dedicated sports fan.

Sample sports words

  • Easy: football, tennis, golf, boxing, rugby, cycling, swimming, skiing, judo, yoga, dancing, running, kayaking, surfing, sailing
  • Medium: volleyball, basketball, climbing, fencing, diving, handball, baseball, athletics, triathlon, badminton, skating, bowling, equestrian, rowing, gymnastics
  • Hard: skydiving, pentathlon, weightlifting, trampolining, taekwondo, decathlon, biathlon, bobsleigh, paragliding, waterpolo, archery, speedskating

Educational value

Sports vocabulary is a reliable engagement tool in PE and language classrooms. The international scope of hard difficulty words — taekwondo, biathlon, bobsleigh — introduces students to Olympic vocabulary and global sporting culture.
