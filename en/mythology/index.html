Hangmango's Mythology category is unlike anything you'll find in other word games. Guess the names of gods, heroes, monsters and legendary figures from Greek, Norse, Egyptian and world mythology. A unique vocabulary challenge that spans thousands of years of human storytelling.

How to play the Mythology category

Mythology names often come from Ancient Greek, Norse or Egyptian and contain unusual letter combinations. Look for double consonants, "ch" sounds (Achilles) and rare letters like X and Y. Easy words are iconic figures most people will recognise — Zeus, Thor, Medusa. Medium introduces less-taught but still well-known figures. Hard features obscure mythological characters that will genuinely challenge classical studies enthusiasts.
