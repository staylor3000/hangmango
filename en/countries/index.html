Hangmango's Countries category is a geography quiz in word game form. Guess country names letter by letter — from short familiar names to long multi-syllable countries that will challenge even geography enthusiasts.

How to play the Countries category

Country names often end in common suffixes — "-ia," "-an," "-land," "-stan." Spotting these patterns early can dramatically reduce the search space. Try common vowels first, then look for double letters. Easy covers major world countries you'll recognise immediately. Medium introduces less frequently discussed nations. Hard features countries whose English names are particularly long or unusual — Kazakhstan, Mozambique, Tajikistan.

Sample country words

  • Easy: france, spain, italy, china, japan, brazil, canada, morocco, india, egypt, greece, switzerland, mexico, russia, cuba
  • Medium: germany, portugal, australia, argentina, colombia, nigeria, vietnam, ukraine, peru, algeria, romania, hungary, tunisia, pakistan, senegal
  • Hard: mozambique, bangladesh, azerbaijan, madagascar, kazakhstan, guatemala, cameroon, zimbabwe, nicaragua, tajikistan, uzbekistan, ethiopia, mauritania, botswana, slovakia

Educational value

Geography vocabulary underpins social studies and humanities curricula worldwide. This category encourages players to extend their knowledge beyond familiar countries to the full diversity of the world's nations.
