How to play the Animals category

Animal words are great for beginners because they often follow predictable patterns вЂ” many end in common suffixes or contain repeated letters. Start with the vowels A, E, I, O, U, then try common consonants like S, T, R and N. On easy difficulty you'll find familiar animals like cat, dog and horse. Medium introduces zoo animals like giraffe, flamingo and jaguar. Hard difficulty features rare species like rhinoceros, platypus and chameleon вЂ” perfect for testing your natural history knowledge.
