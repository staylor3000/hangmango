Food Word Guessing Game

Play Food Now 🥭

Hangmango's Food category covers everything from everyday ingredients to international cuisine. Whether you're a food enthusiast or using it for vocabulary practice, the Food category offers deliciously satisfying words to guess.

How to play the Food category

Food words often contain double letters and uncommon consonant combinations — think "broccoli," "raspberry" or "croissant." Start with vowels and common letters, then use your knowledge of cuisines to narrow down possibilities. Easy words are familiar everyday foods. Medium introduces international ingredients and dishes. Hard difficulty features complex culinary terms and dishes that will challenge even food experts.

Sample food words

  • Easy: bread, milk, egg, rice, soup, cake, salad, pizza, sugar, butter, apple, lemon, melon, cherry, strawberry
  • Medium: cheese, salmon, prawn, broccoli, pepper, raspberry, aubergine, courgette, croissant, mustard, cinnamon, hazelnut, apricot, mushroom, avocado
  • Hard: ratatouille, profiterole, hollandaise, gorgonzola, bruschetta, carpaccio, bouillabaisse, bechamel, taramasalata, chimichurri, gazpacho, prosciutto, tiramisu

Educational value

Food vocabulary is a cornerstone of language learning at every level. This category maps directly to CEFR A1-B2 food and drink vocabulary lists, making it ideal for language learners and MFL classrooms. The hard difficulty introduces international culinary vocabulary that builds cultural knowledge alongside language skills.
