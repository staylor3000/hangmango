Hangmango's Science category spans the full breadth of scientific vocabulary — from simple natural phenomena to complex scientific terms. Whether you're a student revising for exams or a science enthusiast, this category offers rewarding intellectual challenge.

How to play the Science category

Scientific vocabulary often derives from Greek and Latin roots — recognising prefixes like "photo-" (photosynthesis), "bio-" (biochemistry) and "thermo-" (thermodynamics) can help you guess letters strategically. Start with common vowels, then try S, T, R and N. Easy words are familiar natural concepts. Medium introduces scientific terms taught at secondary school level. Hard features advanced terminology that will challenge even science graduates.

Sample science words

  • Easy: atom, sun, moon, earth, water, fire, wind, rain, snow, root, flower, seed, blood, bone, skin
  • Medium: oxygen, carbon, gravity, eclipse, erosion, photon, neuron, bacteria, cell, isotope, molecule, hormone, enzyme, comet
  • Hard: photosynthesis, ecosystem, chromosome, metabolism, thermodynamics, electrolysis, astrophysics, magnetism, crystallography, biochemistry, neurology, spectroscopy, tectonics, geophysics

Educational value

Science vocabulary is a direct curriculum requirement from KS2 through A-Level and beyond. This category aligns with GCSE and A-Level science terminology, making it a genuine revision tool as well as a game.
